    Fusion Directory

    FusionDirectory is where you can manage your account and password.
    The same username and password are used for: Mattermost, Kanboard, Nextcloud.

    How to change your password:

    1. Go to the FusionDirectory sign-in page. (https://user.emlondon.ca/)
    2. Login to your account page with your existing username and password.
    3. Select “Edit” on the bottom-right Corner of the page. Edit your Password, Select “OK” when finished.
      Click “Sign out” at the top-left side of the page.
