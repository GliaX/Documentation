    Change Filament Spools

    Waste during the 3D printing process has long been a problem. An initiative spearheaded by Richard Horne (aka RichRap) kicked off a flurry of activity that culminated in most major filament manufacturers offering coils of filament without the spools. This video by Thomas Sanladerer is a good explainer.

    3D printed master spool

    The 3D printed master spool can be found on thingiverse, and can be printed in PLA or PETG.

    • https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2869973
    • Locally hosted STLs: [Female] [Male]

    Install coil onto master spool

    See this video for a walkthrough of installation:
